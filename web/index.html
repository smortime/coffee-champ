About

Coffee Champ is a web app for suggesting coffee regions based off your tastes. It was created by Schuyler Mortimer, a coffee obsessed software engineer, as a fun project to play around with different technology. It is currently in the early alpha stages but hope plan to create even more coffee centered services and hope that one day it makes an actual good recommendation.
